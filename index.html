What is "mentioning"?
Often, when you want to get someone's attention in a group chat, you mention them. You do this by typing @everyone or @here, followed by your message. This sends a notification to everyone in the group chat, and the message will appear in their chat window. Also this marks the chat preview as red. So the person you mentioned will see the message and know that you want them to read it.
But, this is not necessary in personal chat. Because, there are only you and me. There is no need to mention. Right?
